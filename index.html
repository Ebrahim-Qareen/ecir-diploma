About This Course

Who it is for

Students with CCNA, CEH, Windows Server and Linux fundamentals who want to work as SOC analysts. We do not re-teach networking or operating system basics — we build on them.

How it works

Every session follows the same rhythm: recap, a real incident story, theory, a live demo, lab building, hands-on practice, an investigation, and homework.

What you leave with

A working SOC lab, your own detection rules, a DFIR collection script, written case reports — and the skills the eCIR exam tests.
